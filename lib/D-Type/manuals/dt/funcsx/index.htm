C/C++ Functions

The following functions render simple text. Simple text is defined as a sequence of individual characters or glyphs rendered in a single direction, using a single style and type. The functions described below do not provide support for advanced typography features or contextual shaping rules. See our notes on the Advanced Typography Structures pages for details on how to render complex scripts.

Function Prototypes

Text Line

Output

  • dtxGlyphsDoOutput — Render a sequence of glyphs to Output as a straight line
  • dtxCharsDoOutput — Render a sequence of characters to Output as a straight line

Getting Information

  • dtxGlyphsGetBound — Get bounding box of a straight line of glyphs
  • dtxCharsGetBound — Get bounding box of a straight line of characters

Text Arc

Output

  • dtxGlyphsDoOutput_Arc — Render a sequence of glyphs to Output as a circular arc
  • dtxCharsDoOutput_Arc — Render a sequence of characters to Output as a circular arc
